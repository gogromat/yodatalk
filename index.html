To this page welcome!
Do you want to see how it is difficult to translate human languages? Even the Grand Master have problems keeping up with the meaning. Try YodaTalk, and see for yourself. Just choose different languages, so the translation will be more Yoda-Talk-like. For now the translation sequence is this: English → Japanese → Swedish → English. Be sure to try out other combinations of languages. YodaTalk is language-independent. It works in other languages, just because translation algorithms are not clever. Be sure to try different input/output languages if you are a multilingual person. And as Yoda said: “In a dark place we find ourselves, and a little more knowledge lights our way.”
