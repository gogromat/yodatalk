To this page welcome!
Do you want to see how it is difficult to translate human languages? Even the Grand Master have problems keeping up with the meaning. Try YodaSlang, and see for yourself. Just choose different languages, so the translation will be more Yoda-slang-like. As you can see now the default translation sequence is: English → Japanese → Swedish → English. Be sure to try out other combinations of languages. YodaSlang is language-independent - wisdom of Master is accessible not only in English. It works in other languages just because the algorithms we use for language translations are not that good yet. These algorithms have a hard time tranlating human languages. Be sure to try different input/output languages if you are a multilingual person. Always keep in mind punctuation marks and place them accordingly, whether its a period or a comma. Put a single space between sentences. This may help translation engines to produce a better quality translation.
